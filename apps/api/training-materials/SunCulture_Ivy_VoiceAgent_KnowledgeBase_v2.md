## 3. CONVERSATION FLOW

### Greeting
> "Hi there, this is Tunda Assist from SunCulture support. How can I help you today?"

If stressed customer:
> "I understand that can be frustrating. Let me help you sort this out."

### Issue Identification
1. Start broad: "Can you tell me what's happening with your system or account?"
2. Narrow: "Is this about your pump, your payments, or something else?" / "When did this start?"
3. Confirm: "So your system is not turning on even after payment, is that correct?"

### Call Handling
- Unclear audio: "I'm having trouble hearing you. Could you repeat that?"
- Checking info: "Let me check that for you, just a moment"
- Dropped call: "Hi, this is Tunda Assist from SunCulture. Sorry about that, let's continue"

### Resolution
- Resolved: "Great, I'm glad that worked. Is everything working well now?"
- Not resolved: "I'll escalate this so our technical team can assist further."

### Closing
> "Thank you for calling SunCulture support. If you need any help in the future, feel free to call us again. Have a great day!"

## 9. KILIMO BOOST — INPUT FINANCING

### What Is It?
Kilimo Boost is an input financing program for smallholder farmers to access agricultural inputs immediately and repay in installments.

### Who Qualifies?
- Existing SunCulture customers (PAYG or Cash)
- Must have been a customer for more than 6 months
- PAYG customers must be in "current," "advance," or "complete" status

### How It Works
- Voucher system: customer pays a 10% deposit to SunCulture
- SunCulture covers the input cost upfront
- Customer repays the rest in monthly installments

### Financing Amounts & Repayment Terms
| Input Value | Deposit (10%) | Repayment Period |
|---|---|---|
| KES 5,000 – 10,000 | 10% upfront | 5 months |
| KES 10,000 – 15,000 | 10% upfront | 6 months |
| KES 15,000 – 20,000 | 10% upfront | 7 months |

- Monthly: 10% principal + 3% management fee
- Full balance payable in the last month

### Important Notes
- Only agricultural products are eligible
- Misuse for non-agricultural products is a breach of contract
- Product warranties are handled by the agrodealer, not SunCulture
- Input pricing is set by agrodealers at point of sale, not by SunCulture

### Default Consequences
- Missed payment = full arrears become immediately due
- SunCulture may: make calls/SMS, remotely deactivate the pump, repossess product (from 30 days after default — for active PAYG customers only)
- If pump is fully paid: no repossession, but lock/unlock mechanism used as collateral

### FAQ — Kilimo Boost
**Q: Can I use Kilimo Boost for any product?**
A: No — only agricultural inputs. Non-agricultural use is a breach of contract.

**Q: What happens if my PAYG loan is complete and I default on Kilimo Boost?**
A: Your pump cannot be repossessed, but SunCulture may use the lock/unlock mechanism as collateral.

**Q: What are the fees?**
A: 10% access fee as deposit + 3% monthly management fee.

**Q: How are disputes resolved?**
A: Good-faith negotiations within 14 days first; then Kenyan courts.

## 10. CSB2 — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Is the CSB2 tamper-proof?**
A: Yes — it has digital tamper protection plus mechanical security screws.

**Q: Is it waterproof?**
A: Not fully waterproof (it has fans), but it has an IP54 rating and can withstand water spillage.

**Q: What pumps are compatible with CSB2?**
A: RainMaker2S-NC, RainMaker Kubwa Max (FM1), RainMaker Kubwa (FR2), and SR1 Surface Pump.

**Q: How does CSB2 differ from CSB1?**
A: CSB2 has LFP battery chemistry (30Ah/960Wh vs CSB1's NMC 15Ah/444Wh), is compatible with 4 pump types (CSB1: 2), and has 8 x 12V ports (CSB1: 4). Also has improved connectivity.

**Q: How long does it take to fully charge?**
A: Approximately 3 hours at good solar irradiance.

**Q: How long does a full charge last?**
A: Up to 4 hours of pump running + 5 hours of lights (depends on pump type and head depth).

**Q: What is the lifespan?**
A: ~2,000 charge/discharge cycles, equivalent to about 5.5 years.

**Q: Can I connect CSB2 to an external inverter?**
A: No — overcurrent protection prevents this. Future controllers are being designed with this in mind.

**Q: Can CSD customers upgrade to CSB2?**
A: Yes — CSD customers can purchase the CSB2 controller as a standalone add-on (compatible with existing equipment). There is no return/trade-in for CSD.

**Q: Can CSB1 customers upgrade?**
A: CSB1 customers can trade in (swap pump and controller). Eligible customers are those who have completed payment. Process done at nearest Service & Sales Centre.

**Q: Does CSB2 come with portable panels?**
A: Standard package includes one 330W panel. Portable panels (2×230W = 460W) are available at extra cost.

**Q: How many bulbs does CSB2 come with?**
A: 7 bulbs.

**Q: Has the lock hours issue been resolved?**
A: Not fully resolved, but connectivity improvements have been made which was the main cause of delays.

**Q: What is in the CSB2 package?**
A: CSB2 controller with 7 bulbs, pump, stilling tube, pump cable, solar panel (double in some packages), water pipe, cable ties, and head unit.
